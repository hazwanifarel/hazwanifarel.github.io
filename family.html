My Family

Enjoying Family Life

This is my small family.
I cannot imagine living my life without my family by my side.
Family is very important and valuable to me. They are something
that should never be taken for granted. Without my family,
a large part of my life and culture would be missing.
They are wonderful people and the greatest gift God has given to me.
My father is a responsible person. Then, my mother is kind-hearted.
She is the one who is very concerned about the needs of our family.
They always want the best for their children.
